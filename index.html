How to Play 26-0

Build the ultimate VFL/AFL all-time team and see if you can go 26-0!

🎰 The Draft

Each round, spin to get a random VFL/AFL team and decade. Select one player or coach from that era to add to your list. Complete all 23 rounds to fill your lineup.

🏉 Building Your Lineup

Fill all 18 positions, bench, and coach. Tap a player or coach to select, then choose their position.

📊 Team Rating

Rated on Gls, Dis, Mks, Tks, HOs, and Br Votes — higher stats win more games. Missing stats from older eras are estimated fairly.
